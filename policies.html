Working together

Policies

Fees, cancellations, confidentiality, and what to expect when working together.

Not a crisis service.

If you need urgent help, call 111. For free mental health support in New Zealand, call or text 1737.

Cancellations and missed sessions

At least 24 hours notice is required to cancel or change an appointment. Late cancellations or missed sessions may be charged the full session fee.

Late arrival usually shortens the session and the full fee still applies.

Confidentiality

Counselling sessions are confidential. Information may only be shared where there is a serious risk of harm, a legal requirement, or where necessary to provide appropriate care.

Michael has regular professional supervision; any information discussed is de-identified.

Between-session contact

Email is the best way to contact Michael for appointments or brief questions. After-hours contact can be made, but Michael may not always be available and replies may come the next business day.

Between-session contact is not a crisis service. If you need urgent support, call 111 or 1737.

Online sessions

Online sessions are available by arrangement. Clients are encouraged to use a private, quiet space and a stable internet connection.

Sessions are not recorded.

Boundaries and relationships

Professional boundaries help keep the work clear and safe. Michael does not connect with clients through personal social media channels.

If any potential dual relationship arises, this will be discussed openly.

Complaints process

If you have concerns, it is encouraged to raise them directly with Michael so they can be addressed together.

If concerns remain unresolved, the New Zealand Association of Counsellors provides a formal complaints process at nzac.org.nz.
