Booking

Book a session

Online booking allows clients to select a session type and an available time. Payment is completed as part of the booking process.

What to expect in your first session

A calm, practical conversation to understand what is happening and what you want to be different.

You do not need to have everything worked out before you begin.

Sessions and fees

Session length and fees are listed in the booking calendar.

If you have questions before booking, email is welcome.

Need help before booking?

If you have questions about fit, availability, or what to expect, you are welcome to get in touch first.
