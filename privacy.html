Privacy

Privacy policy

This page outlines what information is collected, why it is collected, and how it is handled.

What is collected

Information may include name, contact details, booking information, and the content of emails or enquiries. If you become a client, brief session notes may also be kept.

Why it is collected

Information is used to respond to enquiries, schedule sessions, provide counselling, and meet professional or legal obligations.

How it is stored

Information is stored securely using password protected systems and, where relevant, locked storage. Access is limited to Michael.

Retention

Records are retained for the period required by legal and professional obligations and then securely destroyed. Some records cannot be deleted before that period.

Access and correction

You can request access to your information or ask for corrections or deletion where appropriate under the New Zealand Privacy Act.

Third parties

Online bookings are managed through SimplyBook, which processes booking details and payment information. Email providers also handle enquiry messages. These services use their own privacy policies.

Website data

This website does not use advertising cookies. If analytics or other services are added in future, this policy will be updated.

Contact for privacy questions

Email michaelmulthaup@gmail.com with any privacy questions or requests.
