About

Michael Multhaup is an experienced counsellor and educator based in Christchurch. He currently serves as Head of Guidance and Student Support Leader at Hagley College, where he leads counselling services and supports student wellbeing across the school community.

Experience

Michael has worked as a counsellor across educational, community, and clinical settings. His background includes senior guidance roles in secondary schools, leadership of pastoral care teams, and counselling in community and correctional environments. This breadth of experience informs a steady and considered approach to private practice.

Counselling approach

Counselling is not rushed or prescriptive. The work begins with understanding the client's context and concerns, then identifying what support is needed and what change may look like. The approach is collaborative, practical, and respectful of each person's values and circumstances.

Cultural context

Michael has extensive experience working in culturally diverse and bicultural environments. His practice takes account of cultural identity, family context, and lived experience.

Outside of work

Outside of counselling, Michael enjoys sailing, cycling, gardening, and practical hands on projects.
